About Value Chain Markets

  1. Enter an industry, business, or product name in the analysis input field
  2. Click the "Analyze" button to generate the value chain analysis
  3. Explore the interactive treemap visualization showing opportunities, pain points, and optimization tips
  4. Hover over cells to see detailed information

Prompt Engineering

Getting consistent, structured outputs from Azure OpenAI required significant tuning of our prompts. We developed a robust approach to ensure the AI would always return data in a format our visualization could use.

Performance Optimization

We implemented a caching system in our MySQL database to store previous analyses, significantly improving response times for commonly searched industries.

Value Chain Markets emerged from a simple observation: while there are plenty of business intelligence tools available, there's a gap when it comes to visualizing and understanding complete value chains across industries.

Our team was inspired by Michael Porter's value chain model, but wanted to take it further by creating a dynamic, AI-powered tool that could democratize access to this kind of strategic analysis.

Comprehensive Visualization

Interactive visualization of entire supply chains from raw materials to end customers.

AI-Powered Insights

Leverages Azure OpenAI to identify opportunities, pain points, and optimization strategies.
